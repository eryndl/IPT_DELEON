I am Eryn Rae de Leon, a 20-year-old self-taught artist residing in Malabon City. I am currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Information Technology at the City of Malabon University.

My passion for art began at the tender age of six. As a child, I would draw on walls and school papers, which eventually grew into a beloved hobby. Initially, I focused on creating drawings of cartoon and anime characters. When I started formal schooling, my teachers recognized my artistic talent. By the third grade, I began participating in poster-making competitions, not only in my school but also in interschool events and other places.

My elementary years were marked by numerous achievements in various poster-making competitions. This continued into high school, where I further honed my skills and explored different art styles. During this time, I also joined the school’s journalism team as an editorial cartoonist. This role allowed me to showcase my talent and brought me the honor of competing at the Regional Schools Press Conference (RSPC).

Now in college, I continue to pursue my passion for art alongside my studies. During my free time, I still draw and create art, keeping the spark of creativity alive.

What I do!

My work includes sketching and portrait art, where I focus on capturing details and expressions with precision. I enjoy working with various mediums such as color pencils, oil pastels, and pens, which allow me to create vibrant, textured, and detailed artworks that stand out.

In addition to these, I also have skills in acrylic painting, where I explore colorful, and creative abstract art or specifically minimalist abstract art. I also do face painting, combining creativity and attention to detail to produce unique and fun designs that bring joy to others.

Art for me is a way of expressing emotions, telling stories, and sharing ideas. Each piece I create is an opportunity to bring a vision to life and connect with people through creativity.

Educational Background

My educational journey reflects a strong commitment to personal growth and academic development, rooted in Malabon City. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) at the City of Malabon University, where I have been enrolled since 2022.

Before this, I completed my Senior High School education at Arellano University Jose Rizal High School from 2020 to 2022, specializing in Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS).

My secondary education at Concepcion Technical Vocational School from 2016 to 2020 further refined my creative skills.

While my early years at Concepcion Elementary School from 2011 to 2016, laid the foundation for my passion for the arts. Each stage of my academic journey has been a stepping stone in crafting my identity as an artist, blending technical skills with the boundless power of imagination.
