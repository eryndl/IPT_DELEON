About the Artist

I am Eryn Rae de Leon, a 20-year-old self-taught artist residing in Malabon City. I am currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Information Technology at the City of Malabon University.

My Works

I am an artist with a wide range of skills in drawing and painting. My work includes sketching and portrait art, where I focus on capturing details and expressions with precision. I enjoy working with various mediums such as color pencils, oil pastels, and pens, which allow me to create vibrant, textured, and detailed artworks that stand out.
